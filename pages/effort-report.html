Effort Report and Contribution

The table below will be pre-populated with the number of months your effort is spent working towards JHC-sponsored work, and non-JHC-sponsored work.

If this section is not populated, or you think it has the incorrect number of months, please contact the CCOM admin staff to correct this: admin@ccom.unh.edu

In the left column, enter for each JHC research theme, the percentage of the number of months at the top of the column that you expended on that theme. The percentages in this column MUST add up to 100%. The form will not allow you to submit until they do.

In the right column, enter for each theme the number of months of effort not supported by JHC but that you think may plausibly be associated with the theme. Please enter whole numbers only (round to the best of your ability). The total in the right column cannot exceed the total effort at the top of the right column (total non-JHC supported time).
