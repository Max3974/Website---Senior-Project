Create Graduate Education

If you are currently enrolled in a UNH or related graduate program, or you are an advisor of a student in a UNH or related graduate program, please fill out this section. This should only be used for programs that you are currently participating in, or have during this reporting period. Do not report on earned degrees or advised students from a previous evaluation period in this section. If you are and advisor to multiple students, or are enrolled in multiple degree programs, please create a seperate entry for each.

This section is only viewable by you and by the Progress Report staff.

Section notes:

  • Title: This should be the name of the degree program, whether you are a student or an advisor. Ex: "Graduate Program, Earth Sciences, University of New Hampshire", or "Doctoral Program, Ocean Engineering, University of New Hampshire"
  • Education Role: specify with the radio buttons if you are a student or advisor.
  • Progress: specify using the radio buttons if the program has been completed or is in progress during this period.
  • Description of Progress: Use this field to talk about the specifics of the degree program, focus of the research, whether it's Thesis/Project Master's, courses taken or given, and anything else that might be relevant to the program. It is recommended to keep this section somewhat brief; if there are broader topics to discuss where this is concerned, it might make more sense to put it in the narrative.
