Create Future Direction

Break down a clear and concise roadmap for your research and work for the next year. All of us know that circumstances, funding, and focuses change, and that's OK - you don't always have to work on exactly what you outlined for the year ahead - but it's important to provide a sense of the direction of your "ideal" plan is for the next six and 12 months, respectively. This can be done in a narrative style, although in certain situations it is appropriate to list goals.

NOTE: If you have already created and saved your Future Direction and wish to edit it, you need to go to

  • >Progress Reporting >My Progress Report (https://progressreports.ccom.nh/my-progress-report).
  • Ensure correct Reporting Period is selected.
  • In the bottom right of the screen, under Required Report Items click on Future Direction.
  • Click on the gear icon to the right of that section. Select Edit.
  • Make the appropriate changes.
  • Click Save.
