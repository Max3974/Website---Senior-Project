Create Award/Honor

If you've been showered with any awards or honors in this report period, here is the place to list it. Do not enter graduate degrees earned in this section - the Graduate Education section is reserved for that. Examples of awards/honors include certifications (Cat. A Hydrography, technical certifications, CPR, NOAA Safety Certificate, etc.), academic awards (UNH Faculty/Staff Excellence Awards, Nobel Prize, etc.), or state/agency awards (NOAA/USN/USCG medals, Medal of Freedom, Victoria Cross, etc.).

Section notes:

  • Title: name of award or honor
  • NOAA Related: check if any NOAA division/group was involved, in any way, in the act of funding, facilitating, or giving the honor.
  • Tagged CCOM Members: other JHC/CCOM personnel who may have also received the award. Do not tag an individual who did not receive an award/honor. This is an optional field. As you type, a drop-down menu will automatically populate (when you stop typing). If the person’s name appears in the menu, click on it to add them. You may do this for multiple individuals. Do not worry about trying to tag people outside CCOM or JHC. This is not a required field.
  • From Organization: Who bestowed the award/honor upon you? This section will also auto-populate; utilize existing entries if you can, otherwise type in the info and move to the next field. This is a required field.
  • Date: Enter the date of receipt. If the award spanned multiple days, check the box labeled "Show End Date" and second Date field will appear.
  • Description of Award: Write a description here in a sentence or two. Keep this section brief — if there are specifics, or broader topics to discuss where this event is concerned, please put that info in the Narrative section of your report.
