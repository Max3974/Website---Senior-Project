If you were featured in any Outreach media, or participated in any Outreach events, enter them here. Some Seminars and Presentations content may overlap what are considered to be Outreach events, so as a general rule, if they can't be classified by any of those other sections, or the event or media was a JHC/CCOM sponsored Outreach event (such as Ocean Discovery Day), please enter it here instead.

Section Notes:

  • Title: Name of the event or the piece of media.
  • NOAA Related: check if NOAA was involved in any way (other than NOAA OCS being the Center's primary funding agent). This includes any NOAA personnel working with you, visiting you, or doing this for or with any NOAA division or group.
  • Outreach Medium: check off which item the Outreach event best fits.
  • Date: Enter the date. If the event lasted more than one day, check the box labeled "Show End Date" and second Date field will appear.
  • Description: In two or three sentences, describe the nature of the event or piece of media. PLEASE USE THE THIRD PERSON IN THIS DESCRIPTION. Also, if there is a URL to any online content, put it in this section as well.
