This section asks you to list any ATTENDED talks, symposiums, seminars (excluding the CCOM/OE Seminar Series), courses or training (independent of your graduate program), and any other event of note. If you gave a talk/seminar/etc., please utilize one of the other sections in this report.

Section notes:

  • Title: name of the talk
  • NOAA Related: check if any NOAA division/group was involved in any way(other than NOAA OCS being the Center's primary funding agent). This includes any NOAA personnel working with you, visiting you, or doing this for or with any NOAA division or group.
  • Tagged CCOM Members: Enter any personnel here who may have attended with you. Do not worry about tagging people outside of CCOM or JHC. This is not the required field.
  • Organizer: Institution, organization, or person responsible for arranging the event.
  • Date: Enter the date. If the event lasted multiple days, check the box labeled "Show End Date" and second Date field will appear.
  • Funding Source: Who paid for you to attend (if applicable)? This is not a required field.
  • Location: Enter the city, state(if applicable), and the country where the event was held.
  • Notes: Write a general descrition here in two or three sentences. Keep this section brief. If there are specifics, or broader topics where this event is concerned, please put that info in the Narrative section of your report.
