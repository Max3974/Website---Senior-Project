Create Invention

If you've invented something you plan to submit to the Office for Research Partnerships and Commercialization (ORPC) at UNH, or had a role in inventing something during this reporting period which will be submitted to ORPC, enter it here. Software may count as an invented item if you plan to patent or license it, so if you think it's patent-able, talk to your supervisor before filling this section out.

Section Notes:

  • Title: name of the invention
  • NOAA Related: check if NOAA was involved in any way (other than NOAA OCS being the Center's primary funding agent). This includes any NOAA personnel working with you, visiting you, or doing this for or with NOAA division or group.
  • Tagged CCOM Members: other JHC/CCOM personnel who may also be creditied with this invention. Do not tag an individual if they did work on it but cannot be credited with the invention. As you type, a drop-down menu will automatically populate (when you stop typing) - if the person appears in the menu, click on them to add them. You may do this for multiple individuals. Do not worry about trying to tag people outside CCOM or JHC. This is not a required field.
  • Submitted to ORPC: select whether or not your invention has been submitted to ORPC.
  • Date Submitted to ORPC: if it has been submitted, specify the date in the format suggested in the field.
  • Description of Intention: Write a general description here in two or three sentences. Keep this section brief - if there are specifics, or broader topics to discuss where this event is concerned, please put that info in the Narrative section of your report.
